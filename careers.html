We Stand Behind You

Kendrick Realty is based on a simple idea:

Your brokerage should grow you, not the other way around.

Explore how you can grow professionally, financially, and personally with Kendrick.

What is the Kendrick Difference?

Leads

We believe agents should spend their time doing what they love most - selling houses. That’s why we provide every agent with a steady stream of quality leads.

Exclusive Territory

Each agent operates in their own dedicated territory and serves as the one local expert. No fighting with other agents for your leads, because at Kendrick we believe in quality of agents of quantity.

Training and Mentorship

Every agent receives extensive onboarding and ongoing training and mentorship, designed by our PhD level professional trainers to allow you to learn faster, sell more, and deliver higher value to clients.

Intensive Agent Support

To succeed as an agent, you need a team of people working behind you. That’s why every Kendrick agent has a support team helping to grow their business

No Monthly Fees

We believe a brokerage shouldn’t make money unless its agents are making money. That’s why we don’t fix monthly charges for things like desk fees, E&O, training fees, mentorship fees.

Custom Organizational Leads Database

When you work with as many clients as we do, organization will make or break your business. That’s why we have a custom lead management tech platform.
